# KrishiMitra AI - Administrator Control Guide

This guide helps administrators operate the Control Center panel to publish updates, manage mandi prices, moderate threads, and review subscription metrics.

---

## 🔐 Accessing the Admin Panel
1. Sign in with an account having the `admin` role (e.g. standard admin seeded in development).
2. A red **Control Center** link will appear in the sidebar navigation. Click it.

---

## 📈 1. Analytics Hub (Default Dashboard)
The Analytics dashboard compiles:
- **Financial Revenue Tracker**: Total billing earnings accumulated from active Premium subscriptions (Monthly ₹99 and Yearly ₹999).
- **User Demographics**: Count of Free accounts, active Trialing accounts, and Premium active subscribers.
- **Usage Telemetry**: Tracks feature hit frequencies, detected leaf pathogens, and popular crops.

---

## 🌾 2. Government Schemes Publisher
To publish new national subsidies or crop insurance schemes:
1. Click the **Schemes Publisher** tab.
2. Enter the Scheme Title, select a Category, and add eligibility benchmarks.
3. List the benefits (e.g., *"75% financial subsidy on solar pumping systems up to 5HP"*).
4. Enter the required documents list (comma-separated), the official apply URL link, and the submission deadline date.
5. Click **Publish Government Scheme**. The scheme will immediately appear in the **Gov Schemes** search directory for all farmers.

---

## 🪙 3. Mandi Price Feeds Manager
To update commodity rates:
1. Click the **Mandi Price Feeds** tab.
2. Select the Crop Commodity commodity type and select the State.
3. Enter the District name and Mandi Name.
4. Input the Minimum, Maximum, and Average rates in ₹ per quintal.
5. Click **Publish Mandi Rate Log**.

---

## 👥 4. Moderation Board & Users Index
- **Moderation Board**: Shows a queue of all active threads in the Community Forum. Click the **Trash** icon to delete a post that violates community guidelines.
- **Users Index**: List of all registered users on the platform showing their names, emails, roles (farmers/experts), mobile numbers, and active plan types.
