# KrishiMitra AI - Farmer User Manual

Welcome to KrishiMitra AI! This guide helps you navigate and use the platform's features to improve your crop yield and manage your farm efficiently.

---

## 1. Getting Started & Profile Setup
1. Open the application in your browser: **[http://localhost:5173](http://localhost:5173)**.
2. Sign up by entering your name, email, password, and mobile number.
3. Select your preferred native language in the top-right header dropdown. The entire platform (navigation, buttons, and AI advisory reports) will automatically translate.
4. On the dashboard, locate the interactive map. Click on the corners of your field boundary to draw and save your farm size in acres.

---

## 2. Scanning Crop Leaves for Disease
1. Click **AI Leaf Pathology** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Drag leaf photo here** or tap the upload box on mobile to select a photo of the infected crop leaf.
3. Click **Analyze Crop Leaf**.
4. The system will return:
   - **Pathology Results**: Detected disease name, pathogen cause, and confidence score.
   - **Treatment Options**: Organic treatments (e.g. neem sprays) and Chemical emergency treatments.
   - **Pesticide Details**: Recommended medicine name, dosage, mixing methods, safety gear warnings, and how many days to wait before harvesting the crop after spraying.
5. Tap **Read Aloud** to hear the advisory read out in your native language. Click **Download PDF** to save the report to your device.

---

## 3. Optimizing Soil NPK and Fertilizers
1. Click **Soil Chemistry** in the sidebar.
2. Enter the pH level and chemical concentrations of Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), and Organic Carbon from your soil test report.
3. Click **Evaluate Soil Health** to get crop suggestions and NPK balancing tips.
4. Scroll down to the **Fertilizer Dose Calculator**. Select your crop type (e.g., wheat, rice), enter your land size in acres, and click **Calculate Bags Needed**. The system will calculate how many 50kg bags of Urea, DAP, and Potash you need.

---

## 4. Chatting with the AI Assistant
1. Click **AI Consultant** in the sidebar.
2. Type any agricultural question (e.g., *"What is the best pesticide for wheat rust?"*) or select one of the pre-written suggestions.
3. The assistant will answer in your selected language.
4. **Voice Assistant**: Click the floating microphone widget in the bottom-right corner. Speak your question. The system will convert your voice to text, query the AI, and speak the reply back to you.

---

## 5. Reviewing Market Prices & Mandis
1. Click **Mandi Prices** in the sidebar.
2. Select your State and enter your district. The platform lists commodity rates, showing minimum, maximum, and average rates for local crops, marked with government MSP indicators.
